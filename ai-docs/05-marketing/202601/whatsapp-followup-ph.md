## About Product Hunt Pre-Launch Reviews

Product Hunt's review link allows supporters to write reviews BEFORE the official launch. Here's how it works:

**The Strategy:**
1. Share review link with early supporters (Thursday-Sunday before Monday launch)
2. Supporters try the demo and write honest reviews
3. Reviews are held pending until launch
4. Reviews go live automatically when product launches Monday 1:31 PM IST
5. Product starts with social proof from day one = better ranking signals

**Benefits:**
- Instant credibility when product goes live (reviews already there)
- Early engagement signals help Product Hunt ranking algorithm
- Saves supporters time on launch day (just upvote/comment, review already done)
- Shows product is real and used (not just hype)

**Best Timing to Ask:**
- **Thursday/Friday (Jan 30-31):** Gives people weekend to try + review (IDEAL)
- **Saturday/Sunday (Feb 1):** Still good, urgency builds
- **Monday morning:** Too late - you want reviews live when you launch at 1:31 PM IST

**Important:**
- Ask for HONEST reviews (Product Hunt values authenticity)
- Test with 2-3 close supporters first to verify reviews don't publish immediately
- Don't spam - one follow-up message per person/group maximum

## Usage Guidelines

**Pre-Launch Timing (Before Monday, Feb 2):**
- **Best:** Send Thursday/Friday (Jan 30-31) - gives people weekend to try + review
- **Good:** Send Saturday/Sunday (Feb 1) - still time before Monday launch
- **Avoid:** Sending Monday morning - too late for pre-launch reviews to help
- Stagger across groups (15-30 min apart)

**Why Pre-Launch Reviews Matter:**
- Reviews go live when product launches Monday 1:31 PM IST
- Starting with reviews = instant social proof
- Helps ranking algorithm (early engagement signals quality)
- Builds momentum from minute one

**Which Version to Use:**
- **Version 1:** Quick minimal ask (busy people, already engaged)
- **Version 2:** General developer groups, balanced context
- **Version 3:** Close friends/supportive community (direct personal ask + urgency)
- **Version 4:** Technical/React developer groups (architecture focus)
- **Version 5:** Groups that need value reminder (may have forgotten first message)
- **Version 6:** Collaborative groups where you asked for app ideas originally
- **Version 7:** Pre-launch momentum (urgency framing, best for Thursday/Friday send)
- **Version 8:** Deep technical/architecture-focused groups

**Best Practices:**
- **Emphasize "review goes live Monday"** - clarifies why reviewing now helps
- **Keep brief** - they already know about Bodhi Chat
- **Clear ask:** Try demo + write review now
- **Provide direct links** - demo + review link
- **Thank genuinely** - early supporters are valuable
- **Don't send more than 1 follow-up** per person/group

**Avoid:**
- Sending too close to original message (wait at least 1-2 days)
- Repeating all product details (wastes their time)
- Unclear timing ("we're launching soon" - be specific: "Monday Feb 2")
- Guilt-tripping language ("I know you're busy but...")
- Multiple follow-ups to same person/group
- Generic "please support" without explaining the pre-launch review benefit

**Note on Product Hunt Pre-Launch Reviews:**
This feature allows reviews to be submitted before launch. Reviews appear when product goes live on Monday. Test with a few close supporters first to verify behavior, then expand outreach.
